About AUST Abuja

The African University of Science and Technology (AUST), Abuja, is committed to advancing knowledge through quality education, research, and innovation. Our goal is to nurture future leaders equipped with entrepreneurial skills, integrity, and technological expertise to drive sustainable development in Africa.
